About Hidden Timber Farm

Opening in Fall 2023!

BRAND-NEW IN 2023: Hidden Timber Farm is a one-of-a-kind horse pasture boarding facility, tucked away in southwest Johnson County, Kansas ... 4 miles west of Olathe, 2 miles south of Kill Creek Park.


Here's what we offer boarders and their horses:

A Small Herd Size

Our pasture boarding operation is geared toward riders looking for a well-maintained, safe pasture for their trail horses. We offer pasture boarding only. Our operation pays attention to details and always has the best care of the horse in mind.

Excellent Pasture & Paddock

Our pasture features a horse-healthy mix of grasses, surrounded by a safe, beautiful vinyl horse fence. Our over-sized paddock, with its excellent footing, features automatic waterers and run-in sheds with fans. These will keep horses comfortable in all kinds of weather.

Personal Tack Lockers

Your tack is an investment. Our personal keyed tack lockers help you keep your horse's tack and your gear safe, organized, and handy. Each locker has a saddle rack, bridle and halter holders, shelving with a hanging rod, and hooks to store your tack.

Beautiful, Private Setting

This farm is a labor of love, and it shows. You can take a relaxing ride along Kill Creek and on our miles of groomed trails within the property. Enjoy fishing in the stocked pond, riding through the woods, and viewing the birds and other wildlife. Or trailer up and head to nearby Kill Creek Park, Hillsdale Lake, or Big Bull Creek Park.
